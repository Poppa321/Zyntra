# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

adaptive

## Users

Manufacturers and distributors, treated as equally primary — a two-sided B2B wholesale marketplace. Manufacturers list products, manage inventory, and fulfill wholesale orders; distributors discover manufacturers, order in bulk, and track deliveries. Both are business operators working in the app during their workday, not casual browsers, so screens should favor clarity and task completion over persuasion.

## Product Purpose

Zyntra replaces opaque, informal wholesale deals with **direct, tracked, trusted** trade between manufacturers and distributors. Success means both sides can transact with confidence: manufacturers get visibility into orders and inventory, distributors get transparency into what they're buying and where it is, and every transaction is traceable end to end.

## Positioning

Direct wholesale trade, without the middlemen or the guesswork — manufacturers and distributors transact directly, with full tracking and trust built into every step.

## Brand Personality

Confident, industrial, trustworthy. Navy conveys stability and trust; gold conveys value and premium quality. Bold, uppercase display type projects confidence. The voice should read as a serious trade tool, not a consumer app — direct and substantial rather than playful.

## Anti-references

Should not look like a generic SaaS admin dashboard — flat, gray, interchangeable enterprise tooling. Zyntra should feel branded and premium even as a utilitarian trade tool.

## Design Principles

- **Trade, not shopping.** This is B2B wholesale infrastructure, not consumer retail — avoid e-commerce/shopping-cart tropes that undersell the seriousness of bulk trade relationships.
- **Trust is visible, not implied.** Order status, tracking, and inventory state should be legible at a glance — trust is built through transparency in the UI, not just through copy.
- **One brand, two roles.** Manufacturer and distributor surfaces share one confident visual language (navy/gold, bold type) even as their workflows diverge — neither role should feel like an afterthought.
- **Substance over decoration.** Confident and industrial means solid, purposeful surfaces — earn visual weight through type and color, not gratuitous ornament.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA: ≥4.5:1 contrast for body text, ≥3:1 for large text, reduced-motion alternatives for any animation, adequate touch targets (≥44×44pt) throughout.
